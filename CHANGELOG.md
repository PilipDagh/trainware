# Changelog

## Week of May 4, 2026

### New features

**Multiplayer lobby system**
You can now create or join named lobbies from the main menu. Set a custom lobby name, choose a max player count (up to 6), and optionally protect your room with a password. Public lobbies appear in a browseable list so friends can find your game and jump in.

**Mobile touch controls**
Play on the go. Touch devices now display an on-screen D-pad and action buttons (Shoot, Interact, Reload, Bomb, Knife) so you can play without a keyboard.

**Spectator mode and group restart**
After dying, you can spectate other surviving players instead of refreshing the page. When everyone falls or the train reaches its destination, the lobby can vote to restart together.

**Reload mechanic**
Weapons now use a magazine. Press `R` to reload, and keep an eye on your current magazine count alongside your reserve ammo.

**Resource inventory display**
Track your hauled gold, silver, and coal directly in the in-game stats panel.

### Updates

**Refreshed in-game UI**
The HUD has been reorganized for clarity, with cleaner stat groupings, updated control hints, and new overlay screens for the shop, death, and victory states.

**Updated player name entry**
Set your conductor name on the main menu before joining a lobby — your name and color now show up in the lobby player list.

**Pinch-zoom disabled on mobile**
Mobile gameplay no longer zooms unexpectedly when you tap quickly during action.

### Bug fixes

- Restart no longer requires a full page refresh — use the in-game vote-to-restart flow.
- Death screen now correctly transitions players into spectator mode instead of leaving them stuck.
