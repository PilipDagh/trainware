title: Shop and saloon items
description: Stop in town to restock ammo, bombs, and beer items at the saloon, including placeable barrels for the train.
---

Every town stop opens a shop NPC near the station. Walk up to the blue **SHOP** marker and press `E` (or **Interact (E)** on mobile) to open the menu.

## Saloon beer items

The saloon stocks two new beer items alongside the regular gear. Each is tracked separately in your inventory:

| Item | Cost | Stock per stop | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Beer Bottle (Refill)** | $3 | 4 | Single-use refill for an existing barrel. |
| **Beer Barrel (Placeable)** | $11 | 2 | Carry-on barrel you can place on the train for your group. |

### Beer bottles

A beer bottle is a quick refill. When you walk up to a placed barrel and press `E`, a bottle in your inventory tops the barrel up by roughly 8 percent (about 5 sips out of 67).

Use bottles when:

- The group has a barrel running low and you don't want to buy a fresh one.
- You're saving cash for ammo or bombs and just need a few more sips.

### Beer barrels

A beer barrel is a placeable consumable for your whole group. Press `B` while standing on the train to place a barrel at your feet. Anyone in the group can walk up to it and press `E` to drink.

```text
Press B on the train -> Barrel appears at your feet (67 sips)
Press E on a barrel -> Drink one sip
Press E on a barrel with a bottle in your inventory -> Refill the barrel
```

<Note>
You can only place a barrel while you're physically on the train. Barrels stay where you put them as the train moves, so pick a spot teammates can reach during combat.
</Note>

## When to use beer items

| Situation | Item |
| --- | --- |
| Solo run, just need a quick top-up | Beer bottle |
| Co-op run, want a shared resource on the train | Beer barrel |
| Existing barrel almost empty mid-journey | Beer bottle to refill |
| Long stretch between towns ahead | Beer barrel placed before departure |
