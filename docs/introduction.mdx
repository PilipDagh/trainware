Transcontinental Survival is a co-op browser game where up to six players ride the rails across an 1870s American frontier. You manage fuel, fight off bandits, mine ore, and try to keep the train, and each other, alive until the final station.

## What's new

The latest release adds a full multiplayer lobby system, mobile touch controls, spectator mode, and a saloon shop with beer items. See the [changelog](https://github.com/PilipDagh/trainware/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md) for the full list.

## How a run works

- Pick a name and join or create a lobby from the main menu.
- Defend the train as it crosses forests, deserts, and tundra.
- Stop in towns to refuel, restock at the shop, and mine nearby ore.
- Reach 3,181 km to win, or vote to restart if your group wipes.
