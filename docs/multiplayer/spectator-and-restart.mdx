---
title: Spectator mode and restart voting
description: Keep watching the run after you die, cycle between surviving players, and vote with your group to start a new game.
---

Death is no longer the end of the session. When your character dies, you stay connected and can either spectate teammates or vote to restart once the group wipes or wins.

## Spectator mode

When your HP hits zero, the **You died** overlay appears and the camera follows a surviving teammate.

- Select **Spectate Next Player** to cycle to the next living player.
- Continue cycling to find the action you want to watch.
- You stay in the lobby and rejoin automatically when the group restarts.

<Tip>
Use spectator mode to coach surviving teammates over voice chat, or to scout enemy spawns and ore patches for the next run.
</Tip>

## Restart voting

Two situations trigger a vote:

| Trigger | Overlay | Button |
| --- | --- | --- |
| All players are dead | **Everyone died** | **Vote to Restart** |
| The train reaches 3,181 km | **Victory** | **Vote to Restart** |

Each player selects **Vote to Restart**. When the group reaches consensus, the world resets: enemies, ores, projectiles, and barrels are cleared, every player respawns at the train with full HP and an empty inventory, and the run begins again from kilometer zero.

<Note>
A restart wipes player inventories, including gold, silver, coal, beer bottles, and beer barrels. Spend or place valuable items before voting.
</Note>
