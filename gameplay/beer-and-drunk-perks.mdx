---
title: Beer and drunk perks
description: Place shareable beer barrels, gain combat boosts, and avoid the 11-sip explosion threshold.
---

Beer is both a buff and a hazard. A few sips give you faster movement and harder-hitting attacks. Push too far and your character explodes.

## Beer items

Two items are stocked at the saloon shop:

| Item | Price | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Beer Bottle | $3 | Refills a placed Beer Barrel by ~5 sips. |
| Beer Barrel | $11 | Placeable container with 67 sips that the whole group can drink from. |

Both are tracked separately in your inventory and don't affect your gold or silver totals.

## Place a beer barrel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buy a barrel">
    Visit the shop in any town and buy a Beer Barrel for $11.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place it on the train">
    Stand where you want the barrel and press **B** to drop it.

    ```text
    PlayerName placed a Beer Barrel!
    ```

    Anyone in your group can walk up and drink from it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refill with bottles">
    Stand next to a barrel that's below 67 sips while holding a Beer Bottle, then press **E** to top it up by ~5 sips per bottle. A single barrel can carry a crew through a whole run if you keep it topped off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Drink and the drunk perk

Walk up to a barrel and press **E** to take a sip. Each sip:

- Removes one sip from the barrel.
- Adds one to your sip count.
- Starts a short drink cooldown so you can't chain sips instantly.

Sip thresholds:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="3+ sips: drunk perk" icon="bolt">
    +11% movement speed and +20% damage with the pistol and knife.
  </Card>
  <Card title="11 sips: explosion" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Your character explodes and dies. Pace yourself.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

A status message confirms the explosion to everyone in the lobby:

```
PlayerName drank too much and exploded!
```

## Strategy

- **Hit three sips before a fight.** The combat boosts apply immediately at 3 sips and last while the drunk timer is active.
- **Track your sip count.** Sips are cumulative and persist until your character dies or the run restarts.
- **Coordinate the barrel.** Tell teammates how many sips are left so the whole group can plan around the next ambush.
