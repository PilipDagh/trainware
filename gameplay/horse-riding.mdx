---
title: Horse riding
description: Mount riderless horses in the wilderness for a movement speed boost.
---

Riderless horses spawn in the wilderness between towns. Mount one to move faster across open ground.

## Mount a horse

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a riderless horse">
    Look for horses standing without a rider in the wilderness. They appear during travel between towns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Walk up and interact">
    Approach the horse and press **E** (or **Interact** on mobile) to mount it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ride for a speed boost">
    While mounted, your movement speed increases. Use it to chase down enemies, retreat to the train, or close the gap to a far ore node.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to ride

- **Catching the train.** If the train is starting its 8-second departure countdown and you're far away, a horse can save you.
- **Long mining runs.** Cover more ground between coal and ore nodes during a single stop.
- **Escaping ambushes.** Outrun horseback bandits and bombers when your group is low on ammo.

<Warning>
  You're still vulnerable while mounted. Bullets, bombs, and knives all hit you the same as on foot. Don't trade combat readiness for raw speed in the middle of a firefight.
</Warning>
