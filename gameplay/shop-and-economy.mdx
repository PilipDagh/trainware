title: Shop and economy
description: Auto-sell ores at the shop NPC and buy ammo, beer bottles, and beer barrels in town.
---

Towns are where you turn mined ore into supplies. Walk up to the shop NPC to sell automatically and open the buy menu.

## Auto-sell ores on approach

Approaching the shop NPC auto-sells any **gold** and **silver** in your inventory before opening the buy menu, so you don't have to manage cash separately.

| Ore | Sell price |
| --- | --- |
| Gold | $5 each |
| Silver | $3 each |

A confirmation message appears for the seller:

```
Sold ores for $24!
```

<Note>
  Coal is **not** auto-sold. Coal is meant for refueling the train at the coal car. See [Train and fuel](/gameplay/train-and-fuel).
</Note>

## Buy supplies

After auto-selling, the shop menu opens. Spend cash on:

| Item | Price | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ammo | Varies | Adds to your reserve ammo. |
| Beer Bottle | $3 | Refills a placed Beer Barrel by ~5 sips. |
| Beer Barrel | $11 | Place on the train for your group to share. |

Beer bottles and beer barrels are tracked separately in your inventory.

## Money tips

- **Coordinate sellers.** Each player auto-sells their own ores, so spreading mining across the group accelerates cash flow.
- **Keep a coal reserve.** Don't dump every piece of coal into the engine the moment you reach the coal car — keep enough on hand to refuel during the next leg if you find no nodes.
- **Buy a barrel early.** A single $11 Beer Barrel benefits the whole group. Top it up with $3 bottles instead of buying a new one each town.
