---
title: Train and fuel
description: Refuel the engine with mined coal, depart on time, and stay on board while the train is moving.
---

The train is the lobby's home base. It carries the group between towns, and it only moves while it has fuel. Keep the engine fed or the run grinds to a halt.

## Refuel the engine with coal

Mined coal can be turned into fuel at the coal car.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mine coal in the wilderness">
    Use the mining action on coal nodes between towns. Mined coal goes into your inventory and is visible on the HUD.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Walk up to the coal car">
    The coal car sits behind the engine. Approach it and press **E** (or **Interact** on mobile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dump your coal">
    Each piece of coal adds **50 fuel** to the train. Anyone in the group can contribute, so split mining and dumping however you like.
  </Step>
</Steps>

A status message confirms the contribution to everyone in the lobby:

```
PlayerName added 150 fuel!
```

## Depart on time

Use the engine button to leave a town when the group is ready.

- Hitting the engine button starts an **8-second countdown** broadcast to the whole lobby.
- Every player sees: `TRAIN DEPARTING IN 8 SECONDS! GET ON BOARD!`
- Stragglers can sprint back on board before the timer ends.

<Tip>
  Use the countdown to coordinate. Confirm everyone has restocked at the shop and refueled before pressing the engine button.
</Tip>

## Don't step off a moving train

Stepping off the train while it's accelerating, moving, or slowing kills your character instantly. Other players see a status message:

```
PlayerName exploded by leaving the moving train!
```

You can leave the train freely while it's stopped at a town or fully halted in the wilderness.

## Town approach warnings

As the train approaches a town, every player gets a heads-up message before pulling in. Use it to:

- Reload your magazine and stock up on reserve ammo.
- Stash mined ores so they auto-sell at the shop.
- Place a beer barrel before the next ambush.
