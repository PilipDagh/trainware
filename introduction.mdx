Transcontinental Survival is a browser-based, top-down co-op shooter set on an 1870s steam train crossing the American frontier. Up to six players join a lobby, board the train, and work together to mine ores, fight off ambushes, refuel the engine, and reach the next town alive.

## What you can do

- **Play together in real time.** Movement, aiming, shooting, reloading, mining, bombs, and knife stabs all sync between everyone in the lobby.
- **Mine, sell, and resupply.** Mine coal, silver, and gold in the wilderness, sell ores at town shops, and buy ammo, beer, and barrels.
- **Fuel the train.** Dump mined coal into the coal car to keep the train moving between towns.
- **Drink at your own risk.** Place beer barrels for your group, gain combat boosts after a few sips, and explode if you push past 11.
- **Spectate and vote.** Watch teammates after dying, then vote as a group to restart the run.
