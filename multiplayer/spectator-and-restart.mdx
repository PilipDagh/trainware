title: Spectator mode and restart voting

Death isn't the end of your session. After dying you can spectate living teammates, and once the run finishes the lobby can vote to restart together.

## Spectator mode

When your character dies, the camera switches to a living teammate instead of kicking you back to the menu. Use the spectator button (or the spectator action on mobile) to cycle through surviving players.

- You stay in the lobby and continue to receive game updates.
- You can keep watching the run, including ambushes, town arrivals, and final stands.
- Spectating ends automatically when the lobby restarts or the last living player dies.

<Note>
  Spectator mode is read-only. You can't shoot, place barrels, or interact with the world while dead.
</Note>

## Restart voting

Once everyone in the lobby is dead, or the group reaches victory, a restart vote opens. Any player can vote to send the lobby back to the menu with stats reset for another run.

### How the vote works

- Each player can vote once per round.
- The vote passes when **at least 50%** of players in the lobby vote to restart.
- After each vote, every player sees a status message: `Restart vote: X/Y needed.`
- When the threshold is reached, the lobby returns to the `LOBBY` state and player stats reset.

### Reset on restart

When a vote passes, the following resets for every player:

- Health, ammo, and inventory
- Drunk sips and timers
- Position and spawn point
- Death and spectator state

The lobby roster, names, and colors persist so you can jump straight into the next run.
